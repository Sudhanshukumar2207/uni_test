    ABOUT US

    Who are we?

    EDUINFO Educational Consultancy is a skill development organization
    that was established to build an infrastructure for learners & equip
    them with future-ready skills & talent, using the latest pedagogical tools.

    What we do?

    We partner with Universities and training institutions and enable them
    with infrastructure development, and relevant qualifications that are
    recognized and respected by employers all over the world.

    Vision and Mission

    Our vision is to emerge as a leading educational entity delivering what's
    necessary to bridge the skill gap which exists around the learning
    demographic and to make education more innovative and interactive.
